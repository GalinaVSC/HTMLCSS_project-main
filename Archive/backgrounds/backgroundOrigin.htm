Sveta appreciates tulips

Tulips are elegant, vibrant flowers that have captivated hearts for centuries. Originating from Central Asia and later becoming synonymous with the Netherlands, these blooms symbolize love, renewal, and prosperity.

With their graceful cup-shaped petals and a dazzling range of colors—from deep purples to radiant yellows—tulips bring a touch of refined beauty to gardens and bouquets alike. Their seasonal bloom in spring is a much-anticipated spectacle, painting landscapes with bursts of color and signaling the arrival of warmer days.
